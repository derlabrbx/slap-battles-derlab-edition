Slap Battles Derlab Edition – Overview

Slap Battles Derlab Edition is a fanmade Roblox game created by derlab (Roblox: derlab80, YouTube: derlabrbx).
The project began on December 9, 2023.

The earliest version of the game featured:

    A simple box‑shaped lobby

    A free‑model Slap Battles default glove

The game has been updated many times since, although no update logs were recorded until September 21, 2025.
Any information before that date comes from:

    Developer memory

    Badge creation dates

    Gamepass creation dates

    Place creation dates

    Rare YouTube footage of the original game

Development History & Team

For a long period, a player named epiklord worked as a developer on the game.
His involvement began randomly — he joined dadwer (an old friend of the creator), and decided to work on the Gaster Blaster for the Sans glove.

Known facts about early development:

    Sans was the only badge glove at the time

    The total glove count was likely four

    Credits for epiklord’s contributions may not be 100% accurate due to memory gaps

    epiklord eventually quit because the game became inactive

Currently, derlab is the sole developer.
